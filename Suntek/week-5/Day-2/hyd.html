HYDERABAD

Hyderabad (HYD) is the capital of Telangana, India, renowned as a major technological hub ("Hi-tech city") and historically known as the 'city of pearls'. It is a bustling metro with a population exceeding 9 million, blending historic sites like the Charminar with modern infrastructure. It is also widely recognized as a premier destination for food and culture. Key Aspects of Hyderabad: Location & Economy: Situated in Telangana, it is a major center for IT, business, and, in 2025, remains a top-tier city for living, offering robust public transport and infrastructure. Culture & Tourism: Known for its unique, rich cuisine (notably Biryani) and historic landmarks. Airport: Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (GHIAL) connects the city globally, providing comprehensive traveler services. Geography: Features areas like Central Hyderabad, located between the Musi River and Hussain Sagar Lake.
