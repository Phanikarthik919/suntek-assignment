BANGLORE

Bengaluru (formerly Bangalore) is the capital of Karnataka and is widely celebrated as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its role as the nation's leading IT exporter. Bengaluru: A City of Dreams, Opportunities, and Timeless Charm Things Only In Bangalore | Best Of Bangalore | TimesTravel Bangalore: My 2 Favorite Neighborhoods and 7 Great Places (2025) Key Highlights Nicknames: Often called the "Garden City of India" for its lush parks like Cubbon Park and Lalbagh Botanical Garden. Economy & Tech: Home to major tech giants like Infosys and Wipro, as well as national organizations like ISRO and Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL). Culture: A diverse, cosmopolitan hub known for its pleasant weather, vibrant nightlife, and a burgeoning craft-beer and cafe scene. Landmarks: Bangalore Palace: A 19th-century royal residence inspired by Windsor Castle. Vidhana Soudha: A massive Neo-Dravidian building that serves as the seat of the state legislature. Bannerghatta Biological Park: A popular destination for wildlife and safaris on the city's outskirts.
